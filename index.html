LunarCoin

Lunar Coin is a digital payment method that involves the management of transactions with zero transaction fees. An important aspect of Lunar Coin is being eco-friendly due to the decreased amount of energy required, as well as requiring fewer validator nodes. Lunar coins, being energy efficient as well as having minimal environmental impact, will be the digital transaction that you can use whenever and wherever you want.
