Our Mission

We sought out to create a usable form of cryptocurrency, that can be used on a daily basis. Say goodbye to transaction fees and long transaction processing wait times!

Why?

Many traditional cryptos use huge amounts of computing power and energy to process transactions, contributing to enviornmental impacts. Lunar Coin is designed to be sustanable while using only a small fraction of that energy.

Benefits

With unregulated transactions, spend your money securely, without having any restrictions! Our platform supports zero fees for any transactions made.
